About

I have no idea what to write about myself...

Interests

Music, especially ambient and metal (progressive metal, djent), music production (recording, mixing), photography, astronomy, nature, hiking, travelling, gym, Minecraft, PC building, abandoned places, creepypastas, and currently getting into reading!

Why did I make this site?

Like so many others, I'm a little sick of the current state of the Internet. Everything seems to happen inside the same dozen platforms, and the magic I experienced as a child in the late 2000s seems to be lost between algorithms and short form content. I've always liked the idea of claiming a little corner of the Internet for myself, to talk about whatever I feel like, to share photos and to share my hobbies and interests. I also love the idea of documenting my life. I started journaling in late 2023, right after starting university, but I want to record more of myself. For those reasons, I created this place!

Brick's Writing Dump is a name I conjured up a while back, for seemingly no reason, but it stuck with me. I want this place to serve as my personal central hub, where everything else links to. I won't ditch websites like YouTube or Instagram, as I still think there is a place for them, but I want everything to link back here.

What should you expect to find here?

Many things! A few years ago I started a Blog where I wanted to post album reviews. It didn't go anywhere, but now I want to continue that effort here. Other than that, I also want to share my photography, write about my day-to-day life, record old memories of mine, and literally anything else I feel like writing about.
