Interests

Why did I make this website?

Like so many others, I'm a little sick of the current state of the Internet. Everything seems to happen inside the same dozen platforms, and the magic I experienced as a child in the late 2000s seems to be lost between algorithms and short form content. I've always liked the idea of claiming a little corner of the Internet for myself that I can completely customize. I also love the idea of documenting my life. I started journaling in late 2023, right after starting university, but I want to record more of myself. For those reasons, I created this place!

Brick's Writing Dump is a name I conjured up a while back, for seemingly no reason, but it stuck with me. I want this place to serve as my personal central hub, where everything else links to. I won't ditch websites like YouTube or Instagram, as I still think there is a place for them; I just want everything to link back here.

What should you expect from this website?

A few years ago I started a Blog where I wanted to post album reviews. It didn't go anywhere, but now I want to continue that effort here. I also want to archive my photography, write about my day-to-day life, record old memories, and literally anything else I feel like sharing. I even have plans to start posting cooking recipes once they actually taste good… I have so many plans for this website, but I don't know how actively I'll be able to maintain it. I tend to be very inconsistent when it comes to these sorts of things…

This website is coded using a 1080p laptop and a 1440p desktop monitor. For now, I won't make this website compatible with mobile displays.
