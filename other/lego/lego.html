Welcome to the LEGO Archive!

This page serves to document my LEGO collection. For years, I have not paid any attention to any of my LEGO sets. They have been collecting dust, and most of them have missing or displaced parts. Therefore, I wish to rebuild my entire collection in full, finding or purchasing any missing parts and gathering all corresponding minifigures and accessories, and then thoroughly photographing every set as best as I can. It's a simple case of nostalgia, and wanting to see the things I loved as a kid (and still do) in their best form.
