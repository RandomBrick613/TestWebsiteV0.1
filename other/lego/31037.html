→ Personal note

I vividly remember assembling this set. I must have received it as a Christmas gift in 2015 or 2016, can't remember exactly. I put it together on a chilly winter night, at the large dinner table at my grandma's old house. I was in the seat near the old piano and my mom was next to me assembling a set of her own - a small gingerbread house that came as a gift in a large order I received. Good times.

I really like this set. It's small but feels beefy when held. It's sturdy and looks nice, with a nice color scheme. The front side looks especially heavy-duty. The functional winch is controlled by the small gear in the back, and works quite well. The roof and doors can easily be removed for a different look.
