Day 1

We arrived at the Faro airport at 4 am. We were both completely exhausted after a night of little to no sleep. We found our way to our gate and boarded the plane not long after. Surprisingly, we landed in Toulouse nearly half an hour ahead of schedule.

After sitting outside for a few minutes, we located the nearest bus stop and purchased a three-day public transportation ticket, which was a great deal considering the number of trips we were planning on making. We took bus number 31, which was already parked close to the machine, to the tramline, and quickly got on the T1 tram, heading for Palais de Justice.

The closer we got to the end of the line, the more beautiful the architecture became. The boring concrete houses and corporate offices were gradually replaced by the lovely brick buildings the city is known for.
